Crystal Collector!

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

  • There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

  • You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose if your total score goes above the random number.

  • The value of each crystal will be hidden from you until you click on it.

  • Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal along with the random number.
